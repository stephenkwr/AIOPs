## How refunds are issued

Aurora Supply Co. refunds returned items to the **original payment method** used at checkout. We don't issue cash refunds for card, debit, or PayPal orders, and we can't redirect a refund to a different card or account.

## Processing timeline

Once our returns center receives and inspects your item, we approve the refund within **2 business days**. From approval, funds post according to payment type:

- Credit/debit card: 3–5 business days
- PayPal: within 1 business day
- Aurora store credit: instant, same day as approval

In total, expect your refund to appear **3–7 business days** after we receive your return. You'll get an email confirmation the moment the refund is approved, with the amount and expected posting window.

## Partial refunds

If you return only some items from a multi-item order, we refund just those items' price. The original shipping fee (a flat $6.95 for standard orders) is only refunded if the entire order is returned within 30 days of delivery. Large furniture items (sofas, dining sets, patio sets) returned without original packaging or missing hardware incur a **10% restocking fee**, deducted from the refund total — for example, a $250 chair returned without its box would be refunded at $225.

## Gift-card purchases

If part or all of an order was paid with an Aurora gift card, that portion is refunded as a **new e-gift card code**, emailed within 1 business day of approval — it is never converted to cash or credited back to a bank card. Any remaining balance paid by card follows the standard 3–5 business day timeline above.

## Late or missing refunds

If it's been more than **10 business days** since your refund-approval email and the funds haven't posted, contact Aurora Support at support@aurorasupplyco.com or via live chat (Mon–Sat, 9am–7pm) with your order number so we can trace the transaction with your bank or PayPal.
