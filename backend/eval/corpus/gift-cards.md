# Aurora Supply Co. Gift Cards

Aurora gift cards are a simple way to let someone choose their own home and outdoor gear.

## Buying a gift card

Gift cards are purchased from the **Gift Cards** page on our website. They are available in fixed denominations of **$25, $50, $100, and $250**, or you can enter a custom amount between **$10 and $500**. We offer two formats:

- **Digital gift cards** are delivered by email to the recipient (or to you) within a few minutes of purchase, and include the card code and redemption instructions. You can also schedule the email to arrive on a future date.
- **Physical gift cards** are mailed via Standard Shipping at no extra cost and arrive with the rest of our standard delivery window.

## Redeeming a gift card

At checkout, enter the gift card code in the **"Gift card or promo code"** field and select **Apply**. The card balance is applied to your order total, including tax and shipping. You can apply **more than one gift card** to a single order, and if your order total is higher than the combined card balance, you simply pay the difference with another payment method. If your order is less than the card balance, the remaining balance stays on the card for next time.

## Checking your balance

To check a gift card balance, go to the Gift Cards page and select **Check balance**, then enter the card number. Your remaining balance and recent redemption history are shown.

## Expiry and refunds

Aurora gift cards **never expire** and carry no monthly fees. Gift cards are **non-refundable** and cannot be redeemed for cash except where required by law. Note that if you pay for an order using a gift card and later return an item from that order, the refund is issued **back to the gift card**, not to a bank account or credit card.
