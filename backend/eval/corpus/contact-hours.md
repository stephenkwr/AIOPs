Aurora Supply Co. support is available through three channels: live chat, email, and phone. Response times and hours differ by channel, so pick the one that matches how urgent your issue is.

## Live chat
Live chat is the fastest way to reach us and is best for order status, returns, and quick product questions. It's available on the Aurora website and mobile app, Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM Eastern Time, and Saturday and Sunday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM Eastern Time. During these hours, typical wait time to connect with an agent is under 2 minutes, and most chats are resolved in a single session.

## Email
Email support (support@aurorasupplyco.com) is best for detailed issues that include photos or order documentation, such as damage claims or bulk order questions. The inbox is monitored 24/7, but replies go out during business days only: expect a first response within 24 hours on weekdays. Emails sent over the weekend or on a holiday get a response within 48 hours, by the next business day at the latest.

## Phone
Phone support (1-800-555-0199) is available Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM Eastern Time and Saturday from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM Eastern Time. Phone lines are closed on Sundays and on major US holidays. Average hold time is under 5 minutes during business hours; call volume is highest between 12:00 PM and 2:00 PM ET.

## Escalation to a specialist
Tier 1 agents (chat, email, and phone) handle general questions, order tracking, returns, and standard exchanges. An issue is escalated to a specialist team — Damage & Warranty Claims, or Large Order Support — when any of the following apply: the order total is over $500, the item has a reported structural or safety defect, or the issue remains unresolved after two prior contacts on the same ticket. Once escalated, a specialist reviews the case and follows up within 1 business day, usually by email, with next steps or a resolution.
