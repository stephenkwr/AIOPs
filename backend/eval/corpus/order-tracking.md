## Finding your tracking number

Once your order leaves our Nashville or Reno fulfillment center, you'll receive a "Your order has shipped" email containing your tracking number and a direct carrier link. You can also find it anytime by signing in to your Aurora account, opening **Order History**, and selecting **Track Package** next to the relevant order. Orders placed as a guest can be tracked from the link in the original order confirmation email using the order number and billing zip code.

## What each status means

- **Order Confirmed** – payment captured; we're preparing your items (0-1 business day).
- **Processing** – item picked and packed at our warehouse (1-2 business days).
- **Shipped** – package handed to the carrier; tracking becomes active within 24 hours.
- **Out for Delivery** – package is on the local delivery vehicle for that day.
- **Delivered** – carrier scanned the package as dropped off, with a timestamp and, for orders over $150, a photo of the delivery location.
- **Exception** – an unexpected delay (weather, damage in transit, address issue); we'll email you with details and next steps.

## Typical delivery windows and delay causes

Standard parcel items (kitchenware, small decor, camping gear) typically arrive 3-7 business days after shipping. Large furniture shipped via freight carrier takes 7-14 business days and includes a scheduled delivery call 1-2 days ahead. Common delay causes: severe weather along the shipping route, incomplete/incorrect addresses, high-volume periods (holiday weeks can add 2-3 days), and items shipping from two different warehouses arriving separately.

## Marked delivered but not received

Wait 24 hours, as some carriers scan packages as delivered slightly before drop-off. Check around your porch, mailroom, or with neighbors, and look for the delivery photo in your tracking details if available. If it still hasn't turned up, contact Aurora support within 7 days of the delivery date so we can file a carrier trace and issue a replacement or refund.
