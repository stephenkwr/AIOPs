**Return window**
Most items purchased from Aurora Supply Co. can be returned within 30 days of the delivery date, whichever comes first is measured from the date on your delivery confirmation email. Items returned after day 30 cannot be accepted for refund or exchange.

**Condition requirements**
Items must be unused, unassembled (or reassembled back to original flat-pack state where possible), and returned in their original packaging with all tags, hardware, and manuals included. Items showing signs of use, assembly damage, missing parts, or odor (e.g., from outdoor cooking use) may be refused or subject to a 15% restocking fee.

**Starting a return**
Log in to your account, go to "My Orders," select the item, and click "Start a Return." Choose refund or exchange and the reason for return. You'll receive a confirmation email within 1 business day with your return authorization number and shipping label.

**Shipping costs**
Returns due to a defective, damaged, or incorrectly shipped item ship back free — we email a prepaid label. Change-of-mind returns use the same prepaid label, but a flat $6.95 return shipping fee is deducted from your refund. Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5-7 business days of the warehouse receiving the item.

**Exchanges**
Request an exchange instead of a refund during the same process. Once we receive and inspect your original item, the replacement ships free of charge, regardless of return reason. Exchanges are subject to the same 30-day window and condition requirements as refunds.

**Non-returnable items**
For safety and hygiene reasons, we cannot accept returns on: propane canisters, camp fuel, and other combustible/pressurized items; custom or made-to-order furniture; mattresses, cushions, or bedding once the protective wrap has been removed; and gift cards. Items marked "Clearance" or "Final Sale" at checkout are also non-returnable.
