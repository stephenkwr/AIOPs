Aurora Supply Co. ships to all 50 U.S. states from our fulfillment center in Reno, Nevada. Orders placed Monday through Friday before **1:00 PM Pacific Time** are dispatched the same business day. Orders placed after 1:00 PM PT, or on Saturdays, Sundays, and federal holidays, are dispatched the next business day. "Business days" always mean Monday–Friday, excluding federal holidays, and all delivery windows below are counted from the dispatch date, not the order date.

### Shipping options

- **Standard Shipping** — 4 to 6 business days after dispatch. Flat rate of $6.95, and it's free automatically on orders with a merchandise subtotal of $75 or more before tax. Sent via USPS Priority Mail for small parcels or UPS Ground for larger, heavier items.
- **Express Shipping** — 2 to 3 business days after dispatch. Flat rate of $14.95; this option does not qualify for the free-shipping threshold even if the order is over $75. Sent via UPS 2nd Day Air.
- **White Glove Delivery** — used for furniture and oversized items over 75 lb (dining tables, sofas, large fire pits). 7 to 10 business days after dispatch. Flat fee of $49.00 regardless of order size, also not covered by the free-shipping threshold. Delivered through FedEx Freight's home-delivery network and includes room-of-choice placement plus removal of packaging.

### Regional exceptions

Alaska, Hawaii, and PO Box addresses can only receive Standard Shipping via USPS, and should be quoted 2 to 4 additional business days beyond the standard window. White Glove Delivery is not available to these addresses.

### Tracking

A tracking number is emailed to the customer within 24 hours of dispatch for Standard and Express orders. White Glove deliveries are tracked separately, with the freight carrier calling to schedule a delivery appointment 1 to 2 business days in advance.
