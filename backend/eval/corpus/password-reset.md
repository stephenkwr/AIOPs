# Resetting Your Password

If you've forgotten your Aurora Supply Co. account password, you can reset it in a few steps without needing to contact support.

## How to reset your password

1. Go to the sign-in page and select **Forgot password?** below the login form.
2. Enter the email address associated with your account and select **Send reset link**.
3. Check your inbox for an email from `noreply@aurorasupplyco.com` with the subject line "Reset your Aurora Supply Co. password."
4. Click the **Reset Password** button in the email. This opens a secure page where you can enter a new password.
5. Your new password must be at least 8 characters and include one number and one letter. Once submitted, you'll be signed in automatically and any other active sessions on your account will be signed out for security.

## Reset link expiry

The reset link is valid for **30 minutes** from the time the email is sent. If the link has expired, the reset page will display a "This link has expired" message — simply return to the sign-in page and request a new one. Only the most recently requested link is valid; older links are automatically invalidated.

## If the reset email never arrives

- Check the spam or promotions folder first, since automated emails are occasionally filtered.
- Confirm you entered the email address on file for your account — if you have multiple email addresses, try the others.
- Allow up to 5 minutes for delivery, as email providers can delay automated messages.
- If it still hasn't arrived, you can request another reset email, but customers are limited to **3 reset requests per hour** per account to prevent abuse. If you hit this limit, wait 60 minutes before trying again.
- If none of the above works, contact support and we can verify the account and manually trigger a reset.

## Two-factor authentication (2FA)

If you've enabled 2FA on your account, resetting your password does not disable it. After entering your new password, you'll still be prompted to enter the 6-digit verification code sent to your registered phone number or generated by your authenticator app. This code is valid for **10 minutes**. If you've lost access to your 2FA device, support can help you verify your identity and temporarily disable 2FA on the account.

## Account lockout after failed login attempts

After **5 consecutive failed login attempts**, the account is temporarily locked for **15 minutes** as a security measure. During the lockout, the sign-in page will display a message stating the account is temporarily locked and the approximate time remaining. Customers do not need to contact support to unlock the account — access is automatically restored after the 15-minute period, or immediately upon successfully resetting the password.
