Aurora Supply Co. offers three membership tiers so customers can choose the level of shipping speed and savings that fits how often they shop.

**Aurora Free ($0/month)**
Every customer account is automatically enrolled in Free. It includes standard shipping rates (based on order weight and destination), access to seasonal sale events, and standard email support with a 2-business-day response time.

**Aurora Plus ($7.99/month, or $79/year billed annually)**
Plus includes free standard shipping (5-7 business days) with no minimum order size, a 5% discount applied automatically at checkout on all orders, and early access to sale events 24 hours before they open to the public.

**Aurora Pro ($19.99/month, or $189/year billed annually)**
Pro includes free express shipping (2 business days) on every order, a 10% discount applied automatically at checkout, early access to sale events 48 hours before the public, access to Pro-exclusive bundles, and a dedicated support line with same-business-day response.

**Upgrading**
Members can upgrade tiers anytime from Account > Membership. Upgrades take effect immediately, and the customer is charged a prorated amount for the remaining days in the current billing cycle at the new tier's rate.

**Downgrading**
Downgrades (Pro to Plus, or either paid tier to Free) can also be requested anytime from Account > Membership. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle — the current tier's perks remain active until then, and no partial refund is issued for the unused portion of the current cycle.

**Cancellation and renewal**
Plus and Pro memberships auto-renew each billing period using the payment method on file. Members can cancel anytime from Account > Membership; cancellation takes effect at the end of the current paid period, and perks remain active until then. Cancellations must be submitted at least 24 hours before the renewal date to avoid being charged for the next cycle. Aurora does not issue partial refunds for unused days in a cancelled cycle.
