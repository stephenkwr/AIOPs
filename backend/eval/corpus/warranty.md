Aurora Supply Co. backs every product with a standard 2-year limited warranty from the delivery date, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal household use. A few categories carry different terms: solid wood and metal furniture frames get an extended 5-year structural warranty (frame only — not fabric or cushions); upholstery, cushions, and fabric components are covered for 1 year; items with electronic or battery parts (rechargeable lanterns, powered coolers, etc.) are covered for 1 year.

## What's covered
Manufacturing defects such as broken welds, warped frames, faulty zippers, malfunctioning folding or reclining mechanisms, and stitching failures that appear within the applicable coverage period.

## What's excluded
Normal wear and tear; fading or discoloration from sun/UV exposure; scratches, dents, or breakage from misuse or improper assembly; damage from commercial or rental use; mold/mildew from improper storage; pest damage; and natural variation in wood grain, rattan, or stone (these are not defects). Shipping damage is handled separately under our Shipping Damage policy and must be reported within 48 hours of delivery.

## Filing a claim
1. Contact support within the applicable warranty window and provide your order number.
2. Describe the issue and attach 2-3 clear photos (or a short video for mechanical issues) showing the defect and the product's serial/label tag.
3. Our team reviews the claim within 3 business days.
4. Approved claims receive, at Aurora's discretion: a replacement part, a full replacement unit, or store credit.
5. For items valued under $75, we may ask you to ship the defective item back using a prepaid return label we email you; larger furniture items typically do not need to be returned.

## Extended coverage
Aurora Care+ can be added at checkout, or within 30 days of the original purchase, for 15% of the item's price. It extends total coverage to 5 years and adds up to 2 accidental-damage claims (stains, tears, drops) per item over the plan term.
