## Updating your email address
Go to **Account > Profile > Email Address**, enter your new email and current password, then submit. Aurora sends a verification link to the new address — it's valid for 24 hours. Your login email won't change until you click that link. As a security measure, we also send a heads-up notice to your old email address so you'll know if this wasn't you.

## Updating your shipping address
Under **Account > Addresses** you can store up to 5 shipping addresses and mark one as default. Edits take effect immediately for future checkouts, but they won't change the address on an order you've already placed — that's locked in once the order is confirmed. To redirect a package already in transit, contact Support directly.

## Updating payment details
Aurora stores payment methods in a PCI-compliant tokenized vault under **Account > Payment Methods**; you can hold up to 3 cards (Visa, Mastercard, or Amex) at a time, plus a linked PayPal account. Only the last 4 digits and expiry are visible to you or our agents — full card numbers are never stored on our servers. Add, remove, or set a default card anytime at no charge.

## Notification preferences
In **Account > Notification Preferences**, customers can toggle: Promotions & Discounts, Restock & Wishlist Alerts, and Product Review Reminders on or off independently. Order & shipping updates cannot be fully disabled (for delivery accountability) but you can choose email-only, SMS-only, or both.

## Closing your account
Submitting a closure request (via **Account > Close Account** or Support) puts the account into a 30-day "pending closure" grace period, reversible anytime by logging in and selecting Reactivate, or contacting Support. If you want a copy of your order history or invoices, request it during this window — Support emails a PDF export within 5 business days. After the 30 days, personal data is permanently deleted within 10 business days. Order records themselves are retained in secure, non-login-accessible archives for 7 years to comply with tax record-keeping requirements.
